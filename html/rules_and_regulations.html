🏀 Basketball Rules & Regulations

  • Team Composition: 5 players per team on court
  • Game Duration: 4 quarters of 10 minutes (FIBA)
  • Scoring: 2-point, 3-point, and free throws
  • Shot Clock: 24 seconds per possession
  • 28m × 15m standard size
  • 3-point line: 6.75m
  • Free throw line: 4.6m
  • Traveling – Moving without dribbling
  • Double Dribble – Restarting dribble illegally
  • 24s Violation – Failing to shoot in time
  • Personal – Pushing, holding
  • Technical – Unsportsmanlike conduct
  • Flagrant – Excessive force
  • Proper attire mandatory
  • No jewelry or accessories allowed
  • Only APU students/staff allowed
  • Zero tolerance for fighting
  • Size 7 (men) or 6 (women) basketballs only
  • Non-marking shoes only
  • Numbered jerseys required
  • Respect all players and referees
  • No verbal abuse allowed
  • Teams accountable for fans’ behavior
