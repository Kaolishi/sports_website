🧢 Required Attire & Equipment

Stay safe, perform your best, and look sharp on the court with the right gear.

Basketball Shoes

Provides grip and ankle support to reduce injury risk.

Breathable Jersey

Comfortable, sweat-wicking clothing for performance.

Water Bottle

Hydration is key — bring your own water bottle!

Towel & Change of Clothes

Stay fresh and dry after every game or session.

Basketball

Official size 7 (men) or 6 (women) for practices & matches.
