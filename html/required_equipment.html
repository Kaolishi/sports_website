Required Attire & Equipment

To participate in APU Sports Club, please ensure you have the following:

Sports Shoes with good grip

Comfortable Sportswear (Jerseys, Shorts, Leggings)

Water Bottle for hydration

Personal Towel & Extra Clothes

Sport-specific Equipment (e.g., Basketball, socks, compression shorts)

❮ ❯

Sports attire is essential for performance, safety, and comfort. It enhances movement with breathable, moisture-wicking fabrics while reducing muscle fatigue through compression wear. Proper shoes and protective gear prevent injuries, while flexible materials allow agility. Wearing the right attire boosts confidence, ensures hygiene by reducing bacterial buildup, and complies with sports regulations. Additionally, sportswear provides weather protection, keeping athletes warm in cold conditions and cool in heat. Beyond functionality, dressing appropriately can psychologically prepare athletes, improving focus and motivation. Overall, the right sports attire is crucial for both professional and casual sports activities.
